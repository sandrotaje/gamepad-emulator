Testing Mouse-to-Right-Stick Mapping

  1. Activate the Virtual Gamepad Emulator extension
  2. Simply move your mouse within the browser window to control the right stick
  3. Stop moving the mouse to see the right stick gradually return to center

Tip: The right stick axes are indices 2 (X) and 3 (Y) in the gamepad API.
